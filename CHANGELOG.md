# Changelog

## v1.2.0 - 2026-07-15

- Added a stories bookshelf view, toggled from the navbar, for browsing stories as books on a shelf.
- Redesigned the bookshelf with a refined, minimal style.
- Made Book mode fully fullscreen with gesture-based chrome that reveals and hides controls as you read.
- Upgraded the Nuxt stack and related dependencies.

## v1.1.0 - 2026-06-19

- Added Book mode for story pages with a focused fullscreen reading layout.
- Added physical book pagination with previous/next controls, page-flip animation, and flip sound.
- Improved Book mode pagination so full story content renders, chapter headings stay with chapter starts, and chapter separators stay with chapter endings.
- Removed oversized paragraph drop caps for consistent reading text.
